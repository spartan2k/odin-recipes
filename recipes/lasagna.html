Recipe

  • Cook: 30 mins
  • Total time: 1 hr
  • prep time: 30 min
  • serving: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 pacage lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pund shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese
  1. bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes or until al dente; drain

  2. preheat oven to 170 degrees C. in a large skillet over mediu-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer for 5 minutes

  3. In a medium bown, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well. in a 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
